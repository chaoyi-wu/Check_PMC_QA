Caption: 
Histology of normal lung tissue in patients with hamartochondroma. In cases of hamartochondroma, only some macrophages and mucus can be detected in the bronchioli (hematoxylin eosin, original magnification ×15). No signs for emphysema or bronchiolitis could be detected.
Question:  What staining technique was used in the histological examination? 
 
A: Gram staining 
B: Periodic acid–Schiff staining 
C: Hematoxylin eosin staining 
D: Methenamine silver staining. 
Answer:  C: Hematoxylin eosin staining
