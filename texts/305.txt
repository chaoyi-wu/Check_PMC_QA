Barium swallow in a patient with cystic fibrosis following right pneumonectomy. Question:  What did the barium swallow study show in a patient with cystic fibrosis following right pneumonectomy? 
 
A: Deviation of esophagus to the left side 
B: Deviation of esophagus to the right side 
C: Relatively vertical lower esophageal segment proximal to the gastro-esophageal junction 
D: No abnormality observed.
